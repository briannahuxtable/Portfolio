Opus Application is web-based product that extends the services of Opus Hotel beyond its physical location. Our team developed the application concept by an interest in how we could better the customer service that a guest relies on for a great hotel experience. The application enhances the current features on Opus's website, using the lifestyle concierges to allow guests to have a personal friend based on their personality. It provides recommendations for personalized activities and places to eat from the hotels fictional concierge The idea of the application is to allow the guest to be able to experience and embrace the local community with a friend living in the convenience of your pocket. The application acts as though it is a friend, providing you with insider perspective of the city.
