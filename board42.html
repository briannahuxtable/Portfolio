My team was given the task of creating a responsive website for a skateboard company. We created a website that focuses on Board 42 as a local startup. They are a skateboard company who specializes in making boards designed by local artists made out of recycled skateboards. Board42 focuses on bringing handcrafted boards into the hands of the everyday skateboarder that want a flexible worn in board. No more having to break in a new board to get rid of the stiffness, board42â€™s recycled boards already are broken in. Each of the boards are one of kind built from thousands of recycled boards and hand painted by local artists. The idea was to bring together the local art and skateboarding scene to create a community and encourage collaboration.
