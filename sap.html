I have been working on SAP Lumira for over a year now creating a data exploration tool. SAP Lumira is a desktop, web and mobile service tool that can help transform and discover hidden insights or answers in data. This multi device suite is designed to create engaging visualizations with fast data manipulation. My main responsibilities are to design the user experience around the suite, primarily on web and mobile. I design interaction patterns, conduct user testing, create high and low fidelity mockups and also help out with the visual design.

SAP Lumira is still in beta, but you can have look here with a free 1GB trial.
